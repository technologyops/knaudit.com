Welcome To Krishnan & Natarajan

Welcome to K & N , Chartered Accountants, a professional firm where we give importance to three C's - clarity, client-focus and custom built solutions.

Clarity - in what the client needs and what we can offer as solutions. This fitment, according to us is very essential for us to take up any work.

Client focus - An uncompromising focus on servicing the clients with timely and qualitative responses and deliveries.

Custom built solutions - We believe in a very personalised approach towards providing solutions which are 'right fit' to the particular client. We do not believe in 'One rule fits all' theory.

We are driven by values which are very native to us and which designate Indians so naturally. Ethical thinking guides us all the time. We believe in advising our clients to have an approach of 'comply and grow' rather than 'compromise and grow'. Compliance to us is non-negotiable.

Simplicity is what we adopt when it comes to approaching a subject matter. Many a times, we tend to think in complex manner for various problems, whereas there are simple solutions even to address complex situations.

Rigorous practices of professional education drive us and knowledge updation & sharing are part of our daily routines.

We believe that relationship with people are always eternal. We value the various dimensions of our professional journey where we encounter our stakeholders like clients, team members, support staff, statutory authorities, professional colleagues, other related professionals, financial institutions, etc. It is our belief to have an integrated and all-encompassing approach, 'inclusive' as they call it. Whatever we do, will have to make sense to the stakeholders.

Welcome to K & N, and experience our association.
