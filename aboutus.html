About Us

Krishnan & Natarajan is a firm of Chartered Accountants founded in the year 1997 by S. Krishnan in Tuticorin as head office and co-founded by G. Natarajan of Tirunelveli. Started from small and emerging towns, the firm started its growth at early stage itself and the variety of professional assignments carried out by the firm was a testimony to the dynamism and energy levels in the firm. The Chennai branch was started in the year 2003. As any other organisation, which has undergone a transformation due to the last decade's socio-economic changes in our country, K&N, which was a firm from small town, has now grown in size and services and today a multi-services, multi-partner firm, catering to various clients in India and abroad.

Our Vision

To be the preferred audit and advisory firm with an international presence built with best of professional skills and expertise, governed by ethical standards and focus on quality of delivery and solutions to clients.
