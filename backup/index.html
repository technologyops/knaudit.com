Audit Quality Control Policy
 
To conduct the audit assignments by personnel who adhere to the principles of Independence, Integrity, Objectivity, Confidentiality and Professional Behavior .

To recruit personnel who are adequately qualified to perform respective duties and constantly update and upgrade their knowledge and skill set by imparting adequate training.
To assign audit assignments to such personnel who are well qualified and trained to perform such work and invest them with such authority and responsibility so as to ensure meeting appropriate standards of audit quality.

To choose clients whose value systems do not in any way, be different from those of our firm and to have a review at least once in an year of the clientele as to the performance of the audit process and response to the audit process.

To have the audit quality policy and procedures continually reviewed and improved upon to achieve utmost standards of the profession .
